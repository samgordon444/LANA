Mac Desktop App

LANA

A fast, local-first board for thinking in cards, lists, and links. Capture ideas, map connections, and ask LANA to summarize the whole board.

  • Infinite canvas with drag, resize, and snap-to-grid placement.
  • Mix text, links, and images with auto previews and notes.
  • Local LLM chat that summarizes boards and surfaces action items.
  • Autosave + history so you can move fast without losing work.
  • Optional backups to a folder of your choice.
